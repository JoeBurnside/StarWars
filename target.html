Target Audience

The target audience of the site is anybody who is a fan of the Star Wars saga. This is a huge audience because of the amount of video games and literature published on the subject. This will also include a large age range as the first film was released in 1977 and the latest instalment in 2005.

It should also appeal to anybody who has seen the any of the many films and television series that have referenced the Star Wars universe.
