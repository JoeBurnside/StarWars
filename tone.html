Tone of the Site

The site will attempt to match the general tone of the Star Wars saga.

At the point in the storyline I have chosen The Dark side is very much in control. The scene is set inside the Death Star and features the death of a major character.

There are also aspects of danger and excitement as there is a battle using fast and dangerous glowing weapons.
